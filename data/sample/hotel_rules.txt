Hotel Rules and Regulations

1. Hotel Timings:
   - Entry before 9:00 PM for all residents.
   - Late entry requires prior written permission from the warden.

2. Room Cleanliness:
   - Residents must keep their rooms clean and tidy at all times.
   - Regular inspections will be conducted every Saturday.

3. Visitors:
   - Visitors are only allowed between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM.
   - No visitors are permitted in the rooms; meetings must take place in the common area.

4. Food and Cooking:
   - Cooking in rooms is strictly prohibited.
   - Meals will be served in the dining hall at scheduled times.

5. Noise and Conduct:
   - Maintain silence between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM.
   - Loud music, shouting, or disruptive behavior is not allowed.

6. Damage to Property:
   - Any damage to hostel property must be reported immediately.
   - Residents will be charged for repairs or replacement.

7. Prohibited Items:
   - Alcohol, drugs, and smoking are strictly banned inside the hostel premises.

Failure to follow these rules may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion.
